Returns & Refunds

Easy and transparent return policy for your peace of mind

Return Policy

At RevShop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, you can return most products within 14 days of delivery.

  • Items must be in original condition and packaging.
  • All tags, manuals, and accessories must be included.
  • Personalized or digital items are not eligible for return.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, go to your Orders page, find the relevant order, and click the "Request Return" button next to the item. Follow the on-screen instructions to print your return label.
