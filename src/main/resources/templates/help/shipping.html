Shopping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about ordering on RevShop

1. How to Order

Ordering at RevShop is simple and secure:

  • Browse products by category or use the search bar.
  • Click on a product to view details, ratings, and reviews.
  • Add the desired quantity to your cart.
  • Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Provide your delivery address and complete the payment.

3. Delivery & Shipping

We work with reliable logistics partners to get your products to you safely:

  • Standard Shipping: Usually delivered within 3-5 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Available for select locations with delivery in 1-2 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Calculated based on weight and destination at checkout.
