Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping on RevShop

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by navigating to the "Orders" section in your account. Each order level provides real-time status updates from processing to delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

How can I contact a seller?

On every product detail page, you can see the seller's business name. If you have specific questions about a product, you can use our upcoming direct messaging feature or contact our support team to facilitate communication.

Is my data secure?

Yes, RevShop uses advanced security protocols and JWT-based authentication to ensure that your personal information and transaction data remain private and protected.
